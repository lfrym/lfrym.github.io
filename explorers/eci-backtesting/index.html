ECI Forecast Backtesting Explorer

Interactive visualization of how ECI forecasts would have performed at different historical dates

How to read this chart: Blue dots show model capabilities as estimated at the backtest date. Green hollow circles show where those same models end up in today's final fit. Green filled dots are future models not yet released at the backtest date. The blue trend line is fitted on historical estimates; the green trend line is fitted on the same models using their final ECI scores.
